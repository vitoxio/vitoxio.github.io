Doki Doki Literature Club: Essay

if you want the thingy to download click here

Personally, I want to become full time game developer/designer. So, I feel that half of that job is about being able to analyse how those before me were able to create their own works. So later on when making my own, I can apply those lessons into what I make. For Doki Doki Literature Club, I disected its use of the 4th wall, and how it bridges the gap between the player and the game. Moreover, for The Un-BEATable Game, I am taking this 4th wall break into a whole other level, though, I do not want to spoil to much of it just yet.
