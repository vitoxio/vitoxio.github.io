Deer Skull

I personally cannot draw, and I still beleive I can't. However, for my first assignement for LMC3402, I was tasked on drawing... Thus after spending days doing sketches I was finally to produce this. The point of it is to slowly take away detail from an image until it reaches its logical extreme.

Car Nuke

❮ ❯

In most action car movies posters, the car is mostly always driving into the camera. This project for LMC 3402 was a test to see, using multiple perspectives and painted textures, how a car could look badass while driving away from the camera. In here you have a 1967 Pontiac GTO driving into a nuke.

Princess Bride

❮ ❯

For this assignement I took inspiration from Youtuver Acerola to create an image with a reduced color spectrum. Additionally, I had to marry the pictures to a movie from the criterion collection. Thus getting inspiration from cinemas multiple movie posters for a single film I decided to create these set of three posters highlighting the actors and characters of The Princess Bride.
