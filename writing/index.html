The Preservation of Retro Video Games via Emulation

In this more academic paper I had to do for my Computer Ethics class I speak about the legality and ethical dilemna of Game Emulation. And how games not being able to be preserved creates a big problem for culture. Consequently I explain a wacky idea that could allow the rental of ROM's similar to how Google Books and the internt archive works.
