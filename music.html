About Music

"Music is shorthand for emotion" - Leo Tolstoy. This short quote shows how hearing something as simple as resonating frequencies can stir an emotion into someone. And that is one of the fundamentals in my music, catchy melodic music that exudes emotion. I mainly sing(Baritone), play piano and guitar. In here I host, the music I have composed, for both my game, and for fun. Noteably, I got into music thanks to the great work of Koji Kondo, specifically his work in The Legend of Zelda series. I basically learned piano by playing Lost Woods in the school piano. I got my sheet music from herbalcell.com , which I don't know where I'd be without it. Enjoy the music!

Reverie

This piece is reminicent of a certain feeling. It is about reminicing about something so much, that one gets lost in the thought. aka, a Reverie. This song tries to evoke this feeling, with a both a feeling of nostalgia, as well as a darker use of the dream, as not daydreams are happy. Reverie (Musescore link)

Project Neon Battle Theme

This is the first track from my upcoming game, The Un-BEATable Game. I decided to keep the current name of the video, as Project Neon was the working name of the title. This catchy tune is, similarly to the tracks in Undertale, is made to be played in repeat. Having a catchy tune to bob your head to as one defeats an onslaught of enemies. As a whole, I limited myself to only 3 notes being played at a time. This was for two reasons. First, to mantain the retro aesthetic, as old 8-bit game only had the ability to play 3 notes at a time, which forced developers to create catchier and more memorable tunes. Secondly, in the game, enemies actually shoot the notes in the music, so to avoid too much cacophany inside the bullet-hell, I decided to keep it at three.
