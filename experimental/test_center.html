ALPHABET

❮ ❯

Engine: GameMaker Studio 2 || When did I do this? July 2020.

What is this? ALPHABET was made for the GMTK 2020 Game Jam (ranked #434 out of 5477), whose theme was: Out of Control. And it is a fusion between twister and snake, in which each letter you grab changes your keybinds.

Design: The game fits the chaotic nature, as it fights the players inate muscle memory as keybinds are completely changed and morrover, the contortion on they keyboard itself. Moreover to the games arcady nature. The player is incited to replay and fight these reflexes.

Code: I coded ...

Cool Fact: as the player obtains letters, musical notes begin to play. Most players do not understand the nature of this, due to their lack of speed and thus the understanding of the music. However, the song that is playing is twinkle twinkle little star, which most people don't know is the same song as the alphabet, hence the games name.
